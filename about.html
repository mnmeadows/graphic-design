About Me

Hi there! I am from Monument, Colorado, but am currently going to school in St. Charles, Missouri. I am studying acting and graphic design at Lindenwood University. I have always enjoyed drawing and being artistic, and felt so cool when I took classes on how to design with the Adobe suite in high school. For the longest time, I tried to deny the computer programmer in my genes, but once I got to college and wrote my first line of code, I was obsessed with building websites. Now, I've built a website for pretty much everything I do, plus one to link them all together so they are all easy to get to (shout-out to my mom for the hours of debugging phone calls). I have found that I also love any opportunity to combine photography with my designs. Truth be told, I feel like I am still working to find my place in the graphic design world, but along the way I have been trying out anything and everything I can to experience every aspect and find my niche. Though that is not doing much to narrow things down at the moment since I have so far just found more things that I enjoy working on, for better or worse. Anyway, if you would like to join me on that journey, feel free to contact me and/or keep checking in here. If you want to know what I'm up to when I'm not sitting in front of the computer, check out my social media or visit home.marissameadows.com to see what else I get myself into.
