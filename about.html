About Me

Hi there! I'm here as your one-stop shop designer! I love continually learning new things, and through that I've done the opposite of finding a niche and just like to do a little bit of everything. For the longest time, I tried to deny the computer programmer in my genes (literally my whole family), but once I got to college and wrote my first line of code, I was obsessed with building websites. Now, I've built a website for pretty much everything I do, plus one to link them all together so they are all easy to get to (shout-out to my mom for the hours of debugging phone calls). I will jump on any opportunity I get to use all of my skills at once and integrate my art or photography into my designs, knowing that I can bring every part of who I am and what inspires me together. I graduated from Lindenwood University with degrees in Acting and Graphic Design and a Certification in Adobe Illustrator, and am currently still based out of St. Charles, MO. If you want to come along for the journey and see what else I can do, feel free to keep checking in here, and/or contact me via email or Instagram. If you're interested in learning more about what I do when I take off my designer hat, you can check out home.marissameadows.com
